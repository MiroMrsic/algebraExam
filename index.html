About Santorini

Santorini is a volcanic island in the Aegean Sea, famous for its dramatic views, stunning sunsets, white houses, and crystal-clear waters. Located in the southern part of the Cyclades, this island was formed as a result of one of the largest volcanic eruptions in history.

With its characteristic white houses with blue domes, narrow streets, and spectacular caldera views, Santorini is the perfect destination for a romantic getaway or an unforgettable experience.

Useful Tips for Visitors

üí° Book in Advance

Hotels and restaurants fill up quickly, especially during summer months. Book accommodations and restaurant tables at least a month in advance.

The best way to explore the island is on two wheels. ATVs provide access to hidden beaches and locations.

üëü Comfortable Footwear

Streets are cobblestoned and sometimes steep. Be sure to bring comfortable walking shoes, avoid high heels.

Arrive in Oia at least an hour before sunset to find a good spot. Alternatives are watching the sunset from Fira or Imerovigli.

Dine at local tavernas away from the caldera for more authentic and affordable food. Locals know where the best food is!
